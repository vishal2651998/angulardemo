FUSER FOR ELECTRICIANS

Fuser is at the forefront of revolutionizing DC Fast Charger service and support. With the rapid, nationwide rollout of these chargers, the industry faces a critical need to ensure uptime and reliability. Our mission is to connect the trained electricians with service and support opportunities in the fast-growing EV Charger sector.

By registering with Fuser, electricians play a vital role in meeting EV charger uptime requirements. We are forging strong partnerships with charger manufacturers and CPOs, streamlining the process of service request to registered electricians on the Fuser platform. Together, we drive the expansion and maintenance of the electric vehicle charging infrastructure, propelling us towards a more sustainable and electrified future.
